Official
🏛️
Official Government Practice Test
The official Tasmanian Government motorcycle DKT practice test. The question wording, diagrams and answer options are identical to what you will see on the real test at Service Tasmania — worth doing a few times so nothing surprises you on the day.
⚠️ Not mobile friendly — best used on a desktop or laptop. Opens in a new tab.
Our Practice App
🗂️
Practice by Topic
Questions organised into 6 topic areas: Line Markings, Intersections, Speed Limits, Vulnerable Road Users, Vehicle Safety, and Motorcycle Rules. Instant feedback on every answer with a plain-English explanation and a link to the relevant section of the official handbook. Includes motorcycle-specific questions. Works on mobile.
Mock Exam
📋
Mock 35-Question Exam
Simulates the real test format: 35 questions drawn across 5 sections (Compulsory, Traffic Rules, Road Rules, Road Safety, Motorcycle), each with its own pass threshold. Instant feedback with explanations after each answer.
⚠️ The general questions are assigned to sections approximately and may not perfectly match the official test pools. The Motorcycle section is accurate.
External
🖥️
EzLicence Practice Test
A well-made third-party practice test that closely mimics the real DKT format. Good for extra practice. Note: requires a free account to use, does not include motorcycle-specific questions, and does not show explanations for correct or incorrect answers.
ℹ️ Free account required — opens in a new tab. Not affiliated with this app or the Tasmanian Government.
